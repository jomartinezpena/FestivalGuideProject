Day 3

Dec 10th (11 am - 3pm): Candlelight Walk

Conclude with a meaningful candlelight procession. Attendees will carry candles or low-light lanterns as they walk to the festival area, symbolizing a return to softer, natural lighting. This procession will help create a serene atmosphere, encouraging attendees to reflect on the beauty of natural darkness and inspiring them to protect it.
