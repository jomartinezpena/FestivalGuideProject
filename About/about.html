About

The Celestial Nights Fest, a delightful three-day adventure celebrating the beauty of natural darkness and the captivating wonders of the night sky. This festival highlights the importance of undering light pollution and its effects on both wildlife and human health, all while supporting the International Dark-Sky Association. With imersive art installations, insightful speakers, stargazing experiences, guided nature walks, and a peaceful candlelight procession, that'll uncover how preserving dark skies can bring us closer to nature and help us reconnect with the stars above.
