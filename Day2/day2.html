Day 2

Dec 9th (5 pm - 8pm): Stargazing and Astronomy Evening

Mention or collaborate with a local astronomy club or observatory to host a stargazing event. The attendees use the telescopes, and binoculars to observe the stars and have guided constellation tours led by the astronomers. Informational talks on how light pollution obstructs our view of the stars and how reducing it could reveal clearer skies.

Dec 9th (8 pm - 9pm): "Nature's Nights" Guided Walk

To emphasize the ecological impacts of light pollution, would be a topic led by naturalists. Allowing participants to observe local wildlife and understand how artificial light disrupts animal behavior. The guides highlighting the importance of preserving natural darkness and provide information about the nocturnal species affected by light pollution.
